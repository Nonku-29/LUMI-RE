Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our treatments and procedures.

What can I expect during my first consultation?

Your first consultation is a comprehensive discussion with one of our specialists. We'll discuss your aesthetic goals, medical history, and assess your suitability for various treatments. We'll then create a personalized treatment plan and answer all your questions. This consultation is complimentary and carries no obligation.

Are the treatments painful?

We prioritize your comfort. Most non-surgical treatments involve minimal discomfort. For injectables, we often use topical numbing creams or very fine needles. Laser treatments might feel like a warm snapping sensation. Your specialist will discuss pain management options with you.

How long is the downtime for most procedures?

Downtime varies depending on the treatment. Many of our procedures, like neurotoxin injections and some fillers, have minimal to no downtime, allowing you to resume normal activities almost immediately. Laser treatments or deeper chemical peels might require a few days of social downtime. This will be thoroughly discussed during your consultation.

How long do the results last?

The longevity of results depends on the specific treatment, your individual metabolism, lifestyle, and skin care regimen. For example, neurotoxins typically last 3-4 months, while dermal fillers can last from 6 months to over a year. We will provide realistic expectations for each procedure.

What are the payment options available?

We accept various payment methods including major credit cards, debit cards, and EFT. Please contact our clinic for specific details on payment plans or financing options if available.
